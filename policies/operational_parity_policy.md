# Operational Parity Policy

Atlas may adapt operational patterns from external agent systems only when the
adaptation stays Codex-native, explicit, advisory and testable.

## Allowed

- read-only reports
- documented handoff envelopes
- evidence indexes
- manual quality bundles
- context resume checklists
- delegation stop rules

## Not Allowed

- `.claude/` runtime directories
- `CLAUDE.md` as a source of truth
- automatic hooks
- mandatory Engram or hidden memory runtime
- deploy automation
- Pixel Bridge or visual office runtime
- installers that mutate user environments
- provider or MCP activation without explicit approval

## Required Boundaries

- Keep Atlas as a reusable factory, not a product runtime.
- Keep adaptations reversible and reviewable.
- Prefer local evidence over hidden runtime assumptions.
- Mark advisory/readiness states honestly.
- Do not claim parity with automation that Atlas has not implemented.
